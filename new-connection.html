If you haven't found the connector you are looking for fill out this form to get started on creating a new connector.

Ideally customers will have their API credentials before filling out this form. Find the API documentation for the software. Follow the steps neccessary to access to your API. You might need to have administrative privileges or make a request to the software provider to gain access to the API. Often you will need to create an application in the sofware. We advise that you name any applications 'precog' so it will be clear what the credentials are being used for in the future. Remember Precog only needs read access to your API.

Having a firm understanding of your usecase for solving a business problem with your data is fundemental. We need to understand what you are trying to accomplish to ensure that we use our time efficeintly and give you the best outcome as quickly as possible.

The more information you give us the better!
