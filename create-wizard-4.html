Step 4

Having a firm understanding of the use case for your data is fundamental. Precog needs an example use case for solving a business problem in order to create the connector.

The connector will not be limited by your use case. You can provide additional uses cases in the future.

Example use case prompts:

  • We need lifetime product value based on marketing costs, orders, returns and fulfilment costs.

  • I need to increase the retenion rate of my placed candidates.

  • We want to rank our staff by how quickly and accurately they process purchase orders and requisitions.

  • I want to monitor off-contract and on-contract spend by department and time.

  • I need to reconcile damaged products and returns with my inventory.
